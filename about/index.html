About Occupancy

Occupancy was created in the hopes of providing a widely available, free solution to help the world follow best social distancing guidelines.

While other occupancy limiting tools already exist, we knew that removing a price barrier and leveraging modern frameworks we could create a useful tool to help the world safely de-quarantine. The CDC has provided a number off tools to help businesses decide when and how to open. We hope this might be a simple tool to also help you feel more confident as you reopen your business, or seek ways to keep ongoing customers safe.

Software developers by trade, we wanted to use our skillset to help in our own little way.

Stay safe out there.
