Some Directions Please

I need to join an existing space

You're in the right place. Enter the space id and password (if applicable) in the form above and click "Join".

Space IDs and passwords can be kinda long though (not to mention the random characters), so we definitely suggest whoever is currently in the space to copy the link and join that way. They can also pull up a QR code for you to scan on your device.


I don't need to join an existing space

No worries, head on back to the home page and start up a new space using the form at the top.
